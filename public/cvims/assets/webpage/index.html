About Us

CABUYAO YOUTH DEVELOPMENT AFFAIRS
City of Cabuyao Laguna

The Cabuyao Youth Development Affairs Office (CYDAO)

was established pursuant to the Executive Order signed by the City Mayor, Atty. Rommel A. Gecolea, and is mandated by Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) Reform Law.

The primary objective of the office is to provide platforms for youth at the local levels for them to participate and play an active role in the conceptualization, planning, implementation, evaluation and monitoring of every youth development programs.
